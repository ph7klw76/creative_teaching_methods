## AI assisted video/audio Generation

There are several 'FREE' toolkits for video/audio generation that can be used for teaching

1. [NotebookllM](https://notebooklm.google.com/)

You need **QUALITY** slides in order to generate such.

For example, it can generate an overview based on the slides you have such as

a) [Quantum Physics and Life](https://youtu.be/BvSnVsv3DIA)

b) [Fundamental aspects of instrumentation and measurement](https://open.spotify.com/episode/7hwDBKEpPn33GMZpcrLRNd)


Another Free with limited minutes per month, you can use [invideo](https://ai.invideo.io/)

a) You need to draft a script (ideally) in  order to it to work better for example 

i) [Unlocking the Secrets of Dielectrics](https://youtu.be/aKxGbzvIq0Q)

ii) Short version:[How Kronig Penney Model Revolutionized Solid State Physics](https://youtube.com/shorts/DQolu06rUf8)

iii) you as an Avatar with scripted speech:[How AI Makes Hard STEM Click: Motivation, Evidence, Strategies](https://youtu.be/TQotAd_ExtQ)
