## Improve FYP before submitting

Often final years projects full of misconception, errors, significant figures that make no sense, lack of explanation and etcs. Students feel safe when AI is blunt and honest

students can ask this questions while uploading the assignment for vetting based on rubrics 

```text
Act as my professor and grade my assignment according
to the rubric. Be very critical. Ensure scientific accuracy and
correctness with citation from reputable journals
```
