## At the end of the class

Often we want to wrap up what the students learned in the 1-2 hours class, you can screen print the slides and attached into the AI and ask the equestion 

```text
Generate 5 multiple choice questions based on the
attached pictures with increasing difficulty and let students
choose the answers and then provide feedback one by
one.
```
